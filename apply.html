Pick the path that fits you, then earn your way in.

Three clear application paths for youth, adults, and people who want to refer someone into the program.

Weekend youth

Who it’s for: young people ready for Saturday labs or APD referral support.

What to expect: hands-on team projects, mentor check-ins, and badge progression.

What you’ll need: willingness to show up, complete tasks, and move up the ladder.

Start your application

Weekday adult workforce

Who it’s for: adults seeking reskilling and employer-aligned workforce training.

What to expect: weekday classes, certification prep, and placement support.

What you’ll need: commitment to attendance, a quick skills assessment, and employer readiness.

Apply now

I want to refer someone

Who it’s for: families, teachers, or community partners who know someone ready to begin.

What to expect: a short referral form, follow-up from our team, and next-step guidance.

What you’ll need: the person’s name, contact info, and a short note on why they should join.

Refer a participant

The earned access explanation

We don’t accept everyone immediately because we respect your time and ours. By earning access, participants show they are ready, committed, and able to benefit from the program.

This makes the experience stronger for learners and more reliable for employers. It is a feature, not a barrier.

FAQ

What does it cost?

The program is designed to minimize barriers. Application is free, and we work with partners to provide scholarships and paid opportunities.

What if I miss a Saturday?

We offer catch-up support and will help you rejoin the next cycle. Consistent attendance is encouraged for progress.

What happens after I earn my silver badge?

Silver badge holders qualify for paid project roles, advanced labs, and stronger employer introductions.

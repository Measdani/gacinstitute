Future Builders Lab

Who it’s for: ages 12–18 in Atlanta, curious about STEM and tech careers.

What happens: hands-on builds, coding, electronics, and mentorship from industry partners.

What participants earn: badge progression, portfolio project, certification prep, and college connection support.

Career outcomes: internship pathways, apprenticeship entry, and high school credit support.

Weekend Track

Who it’s for: youth seeking earned access during weekends, including walk-in participants and APD referrals.

What happens: project-based learning, accountability, and a clear badge progression for paid project roles.

What participants earn: skill badges, digital credentials, and priority registration for higher-level labs.

Career outcomes: stronger school attendance, reduced diversion risk, and early exposure to employer partners.

Learn more

Adult Workforce Development

Who it’s for: adults looking for a direct path into tech-support, facilities, and entry-level engineering work.

What happens: weekday training, certification prep, résumé coaching, and employer-connected placements.

What participants earn: professional skills, workforce badge levels, and referral-ready credentials.

Career outcomes: jobs with local employers, contract roles, and continual earning opportunities.

Brain Break Lab

Who it’s for: anyone in the program needing focus, creative resets, and time to solve problems thoughtfully.

What happens: guided reflection, collaborative challenges, and small-group design sprints.

What participants earn: resilience strategies, improved concentration, and a better pace for sustained learning.

Career outcomes: stronger persistence, fewer dropouts, and a higher chance of completing credentialed tracks.
